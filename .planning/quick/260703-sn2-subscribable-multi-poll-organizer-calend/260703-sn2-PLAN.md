<objective>
Build a shared, subscribable multi-poll organizer calendar feed. Introduce an
account-free "organizer" identity (an unguessable cookie token, `lfg_organizer`)
so a browser's polls group under one token, and expose a webcal/iCal feed the
organizer subscribes to ONCE that lists every finalized poll's booked date and
auto-updates as new polls close.

Purpose: Real recurring value — an organizer adds the feed to their phone/desktop
calendar a single time and every future "Book it" appears automatically, with no
accounts and no participant-data exposure.

Output:
- `polls.organizer_id` NULLABLE column + index (migration 0003, applied locally).
- `getFinalizedPollsByOrganizerId` participant-safe query.
- `buildVcalendar` multi-event ICS builder (buildIcs kept byte-identical).
- `createPoll` mints/reuses the organizer cookie and stores organizerId.
- `GET /feed/[organizerId]/calendar.ics` route (200 empty on unknown/empty).
- Admin-page subscribe card + urls helpers, hidden for legacy polls.

Scope note (orchestrator hand-off): the executor GENERATES the migration and
applies it to LOCAL Docker Postgres only. The prod Neon `db:migrate` + Vercel
deploy are the ORCHESTRATOR's job AFTER this plan — they are deliberately NOT
tasks here.
</objective>
