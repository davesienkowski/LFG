<objective>
Give respondent tracking its source of truth: persist WHO was actually emailed a
participant link (RESP-03). Today `sendInvites` sends invites transiently and
records nothing. This plan adds a NEW additive `invitations` table and makes
`sendInvites` best-effort record each recipient on a successful send.

Purpose: RESP-01 (responded/not-responded tracking) and RESP-02 (nudge
non-respondents) are impossible without a persisted invited list. This is the
data-layer prerequisite, sequenced first.

Output: `invitations` table + drizzle migration; a recording write inside the
existing `sendInvites` ok-branch that never disturbs the UI result rows.
</objective>
