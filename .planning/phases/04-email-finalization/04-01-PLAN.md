<objective>

env-switched `sendEmail()` seam, three plain-HTML templates, a Mailpit local
capture service, an organizer "Invite by email" flow (individual sends, never
CC, per-recipient result chips, graceful copy-link fallback when unconfigured),
and a best-effort VOTE-04 confirmation email fired from the existing
`submitResponse` action.

Purpose: Give the organizer a real send-invites capability and give participants
their edit link by email — the two user-facing email slices this phase promises —
while establishing the ONE seam (`sendEmail`) that Plan 04-02's finalization
emails also ride on. Email is optional by design (D-02): every non-email feature
must keep passing with zero email config.

`src/components/invite-by-email-form.tsx`, optional email vars in `env.ts`, a
`mailpit` docker service, and an `after()` confirmation hook in
`submit-response.ts`.
</objective>
